# QNAPS
QNAPS  ia affordable network-attached storage (NAS) devices. They offer a range of NAS solutions for both home and business use, with a focus on providing high performance and functionality at an affordable price. Their devices run on the QTS operating system, which provides a user-friendly interface and a variety of apps to enhance the capabilities of the NAS.
